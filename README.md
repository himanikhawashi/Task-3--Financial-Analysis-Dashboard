#  Financial Analysis Dashboard using Power BI

##  Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Financial Analysis Dashboard developed using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of financial performance by visualizing key business metrics such as Sales, Orders, Profit, Profit Margin, and Discounts. It enables stakeholders to analyze trends, compare performance across countries and products, and make informed business decisions through interactive reports.

---

##  Objective

To design a professional and interactive financial dashboard that:
- Monitors key financial KPIs.
- Performs time-series analysis of sales.
- Analyzes country-wise and product-wise performance.
- Supports data-driven business decision-making.

---

##  Tools & Technologies Used

##  Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards
  - Total Sales
  - Total Orders
  - Total Profit
  - Profit Margin
  - Total Discount

- Interactive Slicers
  - Year
  - Country

- Visualizations
  - Orders by Country
  - Profit Margin by Country
  - Sales by Product
  - Profit Margin by Segment
  - Discount Distribution
  - Monthly Sales Trend
  - Financial Details Table

---

##  Key Insights

- Total Sales exceeded **92 Million**, indicating strong business performance.
- Germany achieved the highest profit margin among all countries.
- Canada recorded the highest number of orders.
- Paseo generated the highest sales among all products.
- Channel Partners delivered the highest profit margin.
- High Discount Band accounted for the largest share of discounts.
- Monthly sales showed fluctuations with strong growth during the later months of the year.
